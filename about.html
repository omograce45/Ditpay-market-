About DitPay

At DitPay, our mission is simple: Empower every buyer and seller in Nigeria to trade smart, safe and seamless. We are your trusted digital marketplace — where you can buy, sell or discover goods and services with ease.

What we do

  • Provide a vibrant online space where individuals and businesses come together to list, browse and transact items across a wide range of categories.
  • Offer intuitive tools that make posting, negotiating, and discovering a bargain smooth and efficient.
  • Focus on user experience, reliability and trust so you feel confident trading every time.

How it works

For Sellers

  • Register quickly with your email or phone number.
  • Upload clear photos and write a detailed description of your item.
  • Set a fair price, publish your ad and monitor responses in real time.
  • Respond promptly to messages and arrange safe meet‑ups or delivery.

For Buyers

  • Use search filters to find exactly what you need.
  • Chat directly with sellers, ask questions, negotiate where appropriate.
  • Choose collection in person or delivery, inspect goods before payment.
  • Leave feedback after transaction — your review helps build community trust.

Our promise of safety

We believe trade should be straightforward and safe. That’s why we provide verified listings, clear trading guidelines, and 24/7 support for any issues you encounter.

Why choose DitPay?

  • Local focus, global standard — We know Nigeria’s market and bring best‑in‑class platform features to you.
  • Wide variety — Thousands of listings every day across categories and price‑points.
  • User‑driven marketplace — Real people list real items; no middleman markup.
  • Fast and efficient — Post a listing and reach potential buyers in hours.

Our vision

We aim to build Nigeria’s go‑to marketplace, where everyone has the chance to sell what they don’t need and buy what they want — at the right price, with the right people.

Join us

Whether you’re looking to sell something today, find a bargain, or explore new opportunities — DitPay is here for you. Let’s trade smarter, together.
